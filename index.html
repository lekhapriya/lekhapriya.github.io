Lekhapriya Dheeraj Kashyap

PhD student at Texas A&M University with interest in Human-Centered AI and reinforcement learning, with a strong quantitative foundation in stochastic systems, machine learning, and probabilistic modeling. Research focuses on interpretable, learning-based decision models (MDPs, POMDPs, semi-Markov processes) to infer latent states, preferences, and policies from human behavioral data. Experience applying RL and multi-agent learning to human-centered systems, intelligent vehicle systems, finance, manufacturing, power grid operations, and data center operations, with industry experience at Wells Fargo and Hewlett Packard Enterprise.
